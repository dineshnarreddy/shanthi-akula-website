Trauma Recovery

What happened to you does not have to define the rest of your life. Heal at the root — safely, gently, and at your own pace.

Trauma isn't only about big, obvious events. It's anything that overwhelmed your ability to cope — and it lives on in the body and nervous system, long after the mind has "moved on".

A safe, trauma-informed space

My work is grounded in safety, consent and compassion. We never force or re-live pain for its own sake. Instead, we gently help your nervous system feel safe enough to process and release what it has been holding.

What it can help with

  • Childhood wounds and painful memories
  • Emotional flashbacks, anxiety and hypervigilance
  • Grief, loss and life-changing events
  • Feeling numb, disconnected or "not yourself"
  • Patterns of fear that limit your life today

How we work together

We combine psychotherapy with hypnotherapy and neuroscience-informed techniques, always at a pace that feels safe for you. You stay in control throughout — this is your healing, on your terms.

My approach draws on

  • Trauma-informed care
  • Gestalt & ACT
  • Clinical Hypnotherapy
  • Traumatic Stress (AAETS)
