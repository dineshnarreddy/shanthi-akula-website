Questions

You're not the only one wondering

Does hypnotherapy actually work?

Yes — hypnotherapy is a recognised, evidence-informed approach that helps you access the subconscious mind where deep patterns are stored, making lasting change possible.

Do I lose control during a session?

Never. You remain fully aware and in control the entire time. Hypnosis is simply a deeply relaxed, focused state — you can't be made to do anything against your will.

What happens in Past Life Regression?

You're guided into a relaxed state to explore memories and impressions that may be influencing your present life, helping release recurring emotions and patterns.

How many sessions will I need?

It varies by person and goal. Many feel meaningful shifts within a few sessions — we'll discuss a plan that fits you in your free clarity call.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on the service and package. The best next step is a free 20-minute clarity call, where we'll find the right path for you.

Can trauma really be healed?

Yes. With the right, gentle approach, the nervous system can process and release old wounds — so they no longer control how you feel and live today.
