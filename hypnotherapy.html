Hypnotherapy

Reach the subconscious mind — where deep patterns live — and gently create the change your conscious effort alone can't reach.

Much of what shapes our lives — our fears, habits, self-beliefs and reactions — is stored below conscious awareness. That's why willpower alone often isn't enough. Hypnotherapy works with the subconscious, not against it.

What is hypnotherapy?

Hypnotherapy uses a natural, deeply relaxed and focused state to help you access the subconscious mind. In this calm state, we can gently reframe limiting beliefs, release old fears, and install healthier patterns — all while you remain fully aware and in control.

What it can help with

  • Anxiety, stress and overwhelm
  • Fears, phobias and recurring worries
  • Confidence and self-worth
  • Breaking unwanted habits and patterns
  • Sleep, focus and emotional regulation

What a session feels like

You'll sit or lie comfortably as I guide you into a relaxed state through calm, gentle language. You stay aware the entire time — many describe it as the peaceful feeling just before sleep. You can't be made to do anything against your will, and you can open your eyes whenever you choose.

Approaches I may draw on

  • Clinical Hypnotherapy
  • NLP
  • Gestalt
  • Acceptance & Commitment Therapy
  • Neuroscience-informed tools
