Group Psychotherapy

Some of the deepest healing happens when we realise we're not alone. Grow within a safe, supportive circle.

In a carefully held group, you discover that your struggles are shared, your feelings are valid, and change is possible — supported by others walking a similar path.

Why group work is powerful

As a Certified Group Psychotherapist, I create a confidential, respectful space where members support and learn from one another. The group becomes a mirror and a source of strength — often reaching places individual work alone cannot.

What it can help with

  • Feeling isolated or misunderstood
  • Relationship and communication patterns
  • Building confidence and self-expression
  • Emotional regulation and resilience
  • Shared healing around common experiences

How the sessions work

Guided by Gestalt and Acceptance & Commitment Therapy (ACT) principles, sessions are structured, safe and confidential. You share only what you choose to — the pace is always yours.

My approach draws on

  • Certified Group Psychotherapist (IAOTH)
  • Gestalt
  • Acceptance & Commitment Therapy
