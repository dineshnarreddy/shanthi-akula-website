# Shanthi Akula Website — Client Intake Questions

> Send this to the client. Answers unblock the build. Grouped by topic.

## 1. Business & Brand
1. What is the exact name/title you want on the site? (e.g. "Shanthi Akula", plus any credentials/qualifications to display)
2. One or two lines describing what you do, in your own words.
3. Who exactly do you help? (your ideal client — age, situation, what they're struggling with)
4. Do you have a logo? If yes, please send the file(s). If no, do you want one designed?
5. Do you have brand colours or is the wellness palette (forest green / sage / beige / ivory / gold) good to go with?

## 2. Domain & Accounts
6. Do you own a domain name, or should we register a new one? What name do you want? (e.g. shanthiakula.com)
7. Who currently manages shanthiakula.graphy.com — do we need anything migrated from it?
8. Google Business Profile — do you have one? (needed for Google Maps, Reviews, and Local Business SEO) Please share access or the business address/phone.
9. Calendly (or similar) account for booking — existing link, or should we set one up? Confirm the clarity call length (brief says 20 min).
10. WhatsApp number to use for the "chat" button.
11. Google Analytics, Search Console, Meta (Facebook) Pixel — do any already exist, or set up fresh?

## 3. Services
12. Full list of services with the exact names you want (e.g. Hypnotherapy, Past Life Regression Therapy, Neuroscience-informed Healing — confirm/add).
13. For each service: 3–5 sentences describing it, who it's for, and what results people get.
14. Pricing — do you want prices shown on the site, or "enquire / book a call"?
15. How many sessions typically? (for the FAQ)

## 4. Content
16. **Blog:** please export/send your LinkedIn posts (the 100+ articles will be adapted from these). Which format is easiest — a document, links, or a spreadsheet?
17. **Videos:** links or files for (a) videos to embed on service pages, and (b) any video testimonials.
18. **Testimonials:** written testimonials (name + text), and permission to use them.
19. **Google Reviews:** okay to pull and display your Google reviews?
20. **The Client Clarity Guide** — do you have this written already, or do we create it? (this is a downloadable lead magnet)
21. **Self-Assessment Quiz** — do you have the questions/logic, or should we design it?
22. **About page:** your story / journey / why you do this work (a few paragraphs).

## 5. Media Assets
23. Professional photos of you (headshots, working shots). Please send high-resolution files.
24. Any other imagery you want used (space/clinic photos, etc.).

## 6. Contact & Legal
25. Business email, phone, and physical address (for contact page + schema).
26. Social media links (Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook).
27. Do you have Privacy Policy / Terms text, or should we draft placeholders?

## 7. Preferences
28. Any specific pages you definitely want beyond: Home, About, Services, Testimonials, Blog, FAQ, Booking/Contact?
29. Three websites you like the look of (you already gave us reference sites — any others?).
30. Anything you strongly DON'T want (styles, words, colours)?
