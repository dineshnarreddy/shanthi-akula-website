Past Life Regression Therapy

Some patterns, fears and emotions don't seem to belong to this life. Gently explore where they truly begin — and finally set them down.

Have you ever felt an unexplained fear, a deep connection, or a pattern that logic can't explain? Past Life Regression is a gentle, guided process to explore the memories and impressions that may sit beneath them.

What is Past Life Regression?

Using deep relaxation, I guide you to access impressions the subconscious mind may hold — sometimes experienced as memories, images or feelings. Whatever surfaces, the purpose is the same: to understand, release and heal what has been quietly shaping your present.

What it can help with

  • Recurring fears and phobias with no clear origin
  • Patterns in relationships that keep repeating
  • Deep-seated guilt, grief or unexplained emotions
  • A sense of being "stuck" despite doing the work
  • Curiosity about your soul's deeper journey

What a session feels like

You remain safe, relaxed and aware throughout. I guide you with calm, gentle language while you observe whatever arises — without judgement. Many describe it as profound, peaceful and deeply settling.

My approach draws on

  • Certified PLR (Amarantos)
  • Clinical Hypnotherapy
  • Trauma-informed care
  • Neuroscience-informed tools
