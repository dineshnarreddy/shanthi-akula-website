About Shanthi

Healing that begins at the root

A Consultant Psychologist & Healing Therapist helping people in Hyderabad and beyond release what holds them back — and step into a lighter, freer life.

My Approach

Where psychology meets deep healing

For most of my clients, the struggle isn't really the anxiety, the relationship, or the self-doubt on the surface — it's a pattern held deep in the subconscious, often for years. My work is to gently reach that root and help it heal.

I combine clinical psychology and group psychotherapy with hypnotherapy, past life regression and neuroscience-informed techniques. This blend lets us work with both the conscious and subconscious mind — so change feels real and lasting, not just temporary relief.

Above all, my space is safe, warm and free of judgement. Whatever you bring, you'll be met with compassion.
