Narcissistic Abuse Recovery

If a relationship left you doubting your own reality, you are not broken — and you are not alone. Reclaim your sense of self.

Emotional and narcissistic abuse is often invisible from the outside — but it slowly erodes your confidence, boundaries and trust in yourself. Healing is absolutely possible.

You're not imagining it

Gaslighting, manipulation and constant self-doubt leave real wounds. Here, your experience is believed and validated. Together we rebuild your identity from the inside out — with clarity, strength and self-compassion.
